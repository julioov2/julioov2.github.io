Julian's Creator Hub Privacy Policy

Julian's Creator Hub connects TikTok accounts through TikTok's official OAuth flow to support creator planning, account management, and draft upload workflows.

Data we process

The app may process basic TikTok account information granted through OAuth, account strategy data entered by the user, content planning data, and upload workflow metadata.

Tokens

Access and refresh tokens are stored locally on the user's device. Where available, Electron safeStorage is used for local encryption. The app does not collect TikTok passwords.

How data is used

Data is used to connect accounts, prepare creator workflows, support draft uploads, and manage app functionality requested by the user.

Third parties

The app communicates with TikTok APIs only after user authorization. Data is not sold.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the app owner/developer.
