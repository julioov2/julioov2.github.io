Julian's Creator Hub Terms of Service

Julian's Creator Hub is a desktop application for creator strategy planning, account organization, content review, and TikTok draft upload workflows.

Use of the app

Users are responsible for ensuring they have the rights to any content they process, edit, upload, or publish through the app. Users must follow TikTok's terms, community guidelines, copyright rules, and all applicable laws.

Account connections

TikTok accounts are connected through TikTok's official OAuth flow. The app does not ask users to enter TikTok passwords.

Content publishing

The app is designed to support draft-first workflows and user review before publishing. Automated publishing, where available, must comply with platform permissions and API approval requirements.

Contact

For questions about this app, contact the app owner/developer.
